LOCKDOWN KITCHEN



As a kitchen is a place where memories are cooked and seasoned with love , a healthy diet is essential for a healthy immune system. Since the present scenario helped people to bring out the hidden talent where cooking is one of them, we thought of designing a web application project where: People would get the platform to add their delicious cooking recipes with ultimate images and share them globally. Keep a count of their calories in their cooked recipes. They get the opportunity to shop online the ingredients needed in their recipes.
